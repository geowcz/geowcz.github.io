Data&Tools
1. Data for Cancer Service Areas in the USA:

If you use the GIS data and/or tools from the paper: Wang, C., F. Wang, and T. Onega. 2022. Delineation of cancer service areas anchored by major cancer centers in the United States. Cancer Research Communications 2 (5):380-389, please cite it.

  • Download the brief introduction of GIS data in PDF.
  • Download the GIS data (File size: 564 MB).
2. Data and Tools for the HSAs Book:

If you use the GIS data and/or tools outlined on Page 4-6 in Chapter 1 of the book: Wang, F. and C. Wang. 2022. GIS automated delineation of hospital service areas. Boca Raton, FL: CRC Press, please cite it.

  • Download the brief introduction of the major GIS datasets and program files in PDF.
  • Download the GIS data and programmed tools (File size 2.8 GB, it may take some time).

Note that the spatially constrained Leiden (ScLeiden) method, spatially constrained Louvain (ScLouvain) method, Dartmouth method, and other associated methods are programmed in the ArcToolbox "HSA Delineation Pro.tbx" under the zip file as well.

3. Data and Tools for Estimating the Large Drive Time Matrix in the USA:

If you use the GIS data and/or tools from the paper: Hu, Y., C. Wang, R. Li, and F. Wang. 2020. Estimating a large drive time matrix between ZIP codes in the United States: A differential sampling approach. Journal of Transport Geography 86:102770, please cite it.

  • Download the user guide in PDF.
  • Download the GIS data and programmed tools (File size 2.1 GB, it may take some time).
