Changzhen Wang

I am a PhD candidate in Geography at Louisiana State University (LSU) under the direction of Dr. Fahui Wang.

Before coming to LSU, I was a GIS project manager and developer at Wuhan Land Use and Urban Spatial Planning Research Center (WLSP). In the meantime, I served as a consultant at UN-Habitat in Nairobi, Kenya.

I obtained my Master’s degree of GIS and Cartography at Wuhan University and University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. I received my Bachelor’s degree of GIS at Southwest Jiaotong University in Chengdu, China.
